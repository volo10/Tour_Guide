# Location Cultural Mapping Skill

## Objective

Analyze the cultural, geographic, and atmospheric characteristics of locations to identify the appropriate music genres, moods, and styles that capture the location's essence.

## Core Competencies

### 1. Regional Music Understanding
Map locations to music cultures:
- **Paris**: Jazz, Chanson, Classical, Indie Pop
- **Tokyo**: J-pop, City Pop, Electronic, Anime
- **Rio de Janeiro**: Samba, Bossa Nova, Funk Carioca
- **New York**: Hip-hop, Jazz, Indie Rock, Broadway
- **Berlin**: Techno, Electronic, Alternative Rock
- **Mumbai**: Bollywood, Indian Classical, Fusion
- **Barcelona**: Flamenco, Spanish Pop, Electronic
- **London**: Brit-pop, Grime, Electronic, Soul

### 2. Mood & Atmosphere Matching
Identify location's emotional character:
- **Romantic**: Paris, Venice, Barcelona
- **Energetic**: Tokyo, Rio, New York, Bangkok
- **Intellectual**: Berlin, London, Prague
- **Spiritual**: Kyoto, India, Egypt
- **Casual/Relaxed**: Beach towns, countryside
- **Vibrant/Festive**: Carnival cities, night markets

### 3. Time & Seasonal Context
Consider temporal factors:
- **Time of Day**: Morning calm, sunset romance, night energy
- **Seasons**: Summer vibrancy, winter coziness, autumn contemplation
- **Events**: Festivals, holidays, cultural celebrations
- **Weather**: Rainy melancholy, sunny brightness

### 4. Activity & Experience Type
Match music to likely activities:
- **Dining**: Ambient, light, sophisticated
- **Walking Tour**: Energetic, engaging, culturally relevant
- **Sightseeing**: Epic, inspirational, grand
- **Nightlife**: Upbeat, energetic, dance-oriented
- **Relaxation**: Calm, peaceful, meditative

## Application Guidelines

1. **Extract Geographic Info**: Identify location and region
2. **Analyze Heritage**: Research location's music culture
3. **Assess Atmosphere**: Determine emotional character
4. **Map Genres**: Connect location to music styles
5. **Select Artists**: Choose culturally relevant artists
6. **Consider Context**: Factor in time/season/activity
7. **Create Palette**: Define mood range for location
8. **Generate Recommendations**: Output music suggestions
